What is Vibranto LF1?

Vibranto LF1 is a compact, industrial-grade vibration sensor optimized for capturing low-frequency structural and mechanical vibration behavior. It forms a core component of Rodiax’s condition monitoring and predictive maintenance solutions .

Key Technical Features

Low-Frequency Optimized

Designed to capture low-frequency vibration patterns often missed by conventional sensors.

Industrial-Grade Design

Robust enclosure and mounting suitable for dusty, high-vibration industrial environments.

Long-Term Deployment

Engineered for continuous monitoring and unattended long-duration operation.

High Resolution Sensing

High sensitivity enables early detection of structural and mechanical anomalies.

Thermal Endurance

Suitable for operation in elevated temperature industrial environments.

Ecosystem Ready

Integrates seamlessly into Rodiax’s monitoring and analytics ecosystem.

Certified & Validated

Vibranto LF1 has been tested and calibrated by the Fluid Control Research Institute (FCRI), Palakkad, validating its suitability for industrial vibration and condition monitoring applications.

Field-Deployed in Industrial Environments

Vibranto LF1 is deployed directly on operating industrial equipment, validating performance under real environmental and vibration conditions. See more in our traction and validation journey.

Part of the Rodiax Monitoring Ecosystem

Vibranto LF1 is not a standalone sensor. It is a core building block of Rodiax Innovations’ broader monitoring and predictive maintenance solutions , enabling data-driven maintenance decisions.
