Vibranto LF1
Low-Frequency Vibration Sensor

Vibranto LF1 is Rodiax Innovations’ flagship industrial sensing device, purpose-built for low-frequency vibration monitoring, early fault detection, and long-term deployment in real operating environments.

What is Vibranto LF1?

Vibranto LF1 is a compact, industrial-grade vibration sensor optimized for capturing low-frequency structural and mechanical behavior. It enables condition-based monitoring and predictive maintenance by continuously measuring real operating data instead of relying on assumptions or schedules.

Key Technical Capabilities

Low-Frequency Optimized

Designed to capture low-frequency vibration signatures associated with structural degradation and early-stage faults.

Industrial-Grade Build

Robust enclosure suitable for dusty, high-vibration and harsh industrial operating conditions.

Long-Term Deployment

Engineered for continuous monitoring with stable performance over extended deployment periods.

High Sensitivity

Enables early detection of abnormal vibration trends before visible damage or failure occurs.

Thermal Endurance

Suitable for operation in elevated temperature industrial environments.

Ecosystem Ready

Integrates seamlessly with Rodiax analytics, dashboards, and predictive maintenance workflows.

Certified & Technically Validated

Vibranto LF1 has undergone calibration and validation at the Fluid Control Research Institute (FCRI), Palakkad, confirming its accuracy and suitability for industrial vibration and condition monitoring applications.

Built for Predictive Maintenance

Vibranto LF1 functions as a foundational sensing layer within Rodiax Innovations’ broader predictive maintenance ecosystem , enabling data-driven, engineering-backed maintenance decisions.
