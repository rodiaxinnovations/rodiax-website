Rodiax Innovations helps industries move beyond reactive and preventive maintenance by enabling condition-based and predictive maintenance using engineering-grade sensing and actionable insights.

The Maintenance Challenge

Reactive Maintenance

Equipment is repaired only after failure, leading to unplanned downtime, production losses, and safety risks.

Preventive Maintenance

Maintenance schedules are based on time or usage, not on actual equipment health.

Limited Visibility

Decision-makers lack continuous insight into machine condition and early signs of degradation.

Evolution of Maintenance Strategy

Reactive

Failure → Repair

Preventive

Schedule → Replace

Predictive

Monitor → Predict → Act

From Data to Predictive Insights

Predictive maintenance is not just about collecting data. It is about interpreting physical behavior through engineering analysis and converting it into actionable insight.

Data Acquisition

Continuous measurement of low-frequency vibration and operational behavior.

Signal Processing

Filtering, trend extraction, and noise separation to isolate meaningful patterns.

Condition Analysis

Engineering interpretation of vibration trends to assess machine health and degradation.

Predictive Insight

Early warning indicators and maintenance guidance for informed decision-making.

Business Impact

Reduced Downtime

Early fault detection minimizes unplanned shutdowns and production losses.

Extended Asset Life

Maintenance based on actual condition improves asset reliability and lifespan.

Data-Driven Decisions

Engineering and management teams gain objective insights for planning and optimization.
