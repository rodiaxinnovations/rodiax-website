Rodiax Innovations is an industrial engineering and technology company focused on enabling condition monitoring and predictive maintenance through practical, deployable solutions.

Engineering-Led Founding Team

Rodiax Innovations is founded by four mechanical engineers, all alumni of the Cochin University of Science and Technology, with advanced academic and management training from IIM Ahmedabad, IIM Indore, NIT Tiruchirappalli, and Cochin University of Science and Technology. The founding team blends strong mechanical engineering fundamentals with research depth and management expertise, enabling a practical yet rigorous approach to industrial problem-solving. This combination allows Rodiax to design solutions that are not only technologically sound, but also aligned with real-world operational demands..
